Features

Priority

Prioritization is organizing tasks in terms of importance relative to one another.It gives you clarity about which task should be assigned to a team member thereby freeing up your time to do more important work.

Collaboration

Communication is a fundamental element in project management. Collaborating with stakeholders is essential to project success as it ensure that everyone is aware of tasks that are assigned to them.

Time tracking

Time management is an important aspect within a project. Poor time management can have a detrimental effect on the whole project; resulting in a combination of increased cost and poor product quality!
